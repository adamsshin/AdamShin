Phased Roadmap: A Clear Path to Success

We propose a systematic 4-phase rollout to minimize risk and ensure data-driven success, demonstrating our commitment to a true partnership.

Phase 1: Tech Integration & UK Pilot (Months 1-3)

The focus is a 'stable start.' We will launch 5 flagship Instant Games on the lower-risk UK platform (7bet.co.uk) to ensure technical stability and gather initial user data.

  • Key Activities: API integration, launch of 5 flagship games, co-development of initial 'bridge' missions.
  • Success Metrics: Speed of integration, game stability, initial adoption rates, mission completion rates.

Phase 2: Marketing Push & Optimization (Months 4-6)

We 'accelerate growth' based on pilot data. Targeted social media and influencer campaigns will be launched for the UK market, while player data is used to refine game selection and mission difficulty.

  • Key Activities: Targeted marketing campaigns, data-driven optimization, promotional A/B testing.
  • Success Metrics: Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC), retention rates, cross-sell rate.

Phase 3: Full UK Launch & Lithuanian Expansion (Months 7-9)

We 'scale the proven model' to the entire market. The full portfolio will be expanded on 7bet.co.uk, and a phased rollout will begin on 7bet.lt, adapting the strategy for a mature market.

  • Key Activities: Full portfolio expansion in the UK, custom-tailored launch in Lithuania.
  • Success Metrics: Revenue uplift in UK, initial adoption rates in Lithuania.

Phase 4: Full Ecosystem & Continuous Innovation (Months 10-12+)

We become a 'long-term growth partner.' The full portfolio will be active on both platforms, data integration will be deepened for hyper-personalization, and new, exclusive content will be co-developed for 7bet.

  • Key Activities: Full ecosystem activation, deep data integration, co-development of exclusive content.
  • Success Metrics: Blended LTV growth, market share growth in the under-25 demographic.
